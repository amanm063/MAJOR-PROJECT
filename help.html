How Do We Calculate Our Rating?

We compared each and every player to the category best with different parameters being Strike Rate, Runs, Sixes & Fours (for batter) and Number of Balls, Extra Runs and Runs Conceived (for bowler) and then taking the player's mean in respective category.

How to read batter's graph.

1

Top left graph shows the diff values of parameters we selected to calculate the rating of the player.

2

Top right shows the individual rating in each category.

3

Bottom Left shows the total number of balls and runs by the batter.

4

Bottom Right shows the average rating across all fields.
